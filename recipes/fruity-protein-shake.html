Fruity Protein Shake

Description

This protein shake combines the natural sweetness of fruit with the power-packed protein you need, without added sugars or fats. It's designed to be both refreshing and nourishing, providing essential nutrients and a boost of protein to support muscle recovery and energy levels. Made with a base of Greek yogurt for creaminess and a mix of berries for antioxidant benefits, this shake is not only flavorful but also incredibly wholesome. A touch of vanilla extract and optional spices like cinnamon can add depth to the flavor profile, making it a delightful treat at any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop of your preferred vanilla or unflavored protein powder (ensure it's low in sugar and fat)
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt (low-fat or fat-free for lower fat content)
  • 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen, such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • 1/2 banana (for natural sweetness and creaminess)
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk or another low-calorie plant-based milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional, for added flavor)
  • Ice (adjust quantity based on desired thickness)
  • A pinch of cinnamon or nutmeg (optional, for extra flavor)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • If you're using fresh berries, wash them thoroughly. For frozen berries, there's no need to thaw them as they help to chill the shake.
  2. Blend the Base:
    • In a blender, combine the protein powder, Greek yogurt, mixed berries, banana, and almond milk. Add the vanilla extract and a pinch of cinnamon or nutmeg if desired. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.
  3. Adjust Consistency:
    • Add ice cubes to the blender, starting with a few and adding more until you reach your desired consistency. Blend again until the shake is smooth and the ice is fully incorporated.
  4. Taste and Adjust:
    • Give your shake a taste. If you prefer it a bit sweeter, you can add a small amount of a natural sweetener like stevia. Blend again if you make any additions.
  5. Serve Immediately:
    • Pour the shake into a large glass and enjoy immediately. For an extra touch, you can garnish with a few whole berries or a sprinkle of ground cinnamon on top.
